## Invariants and cautions

- INV-13: phones receive only `PlayerDeliveryDTO` projected by the engine and encrypted for their binding. A trystero adapter must have no full-ledger API.
- QR/manual secrets never appear in the ordinary shared layout. The hand-to gate is structural, not CSS hiding.
- A used claim token cannot silently bind a second phone. Explicit replacement and epoch increment belong to the host flow.
- Do not log keys, claim tokens, URL fragments, decrypted payloads, or pairing errors containing them.
- Trystero signaling is serverless, not guaranteed internet-offline. Do not add a backend or claim offline signaling.
- Do not describe transport or at-rest encryption as protection from a deliberately instrumented host.
- Keep the total M2-11 diff below roughly 600 changed lines including tests; split rather than pushing through if it grows beyond that.
