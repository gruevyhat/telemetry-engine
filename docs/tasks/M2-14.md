# M2-14 — Black-box preimage reveal and verification

## Tests first
- property: the artifact's campaign seed and salt verify against the setup-time seed commitment before any draw is checked
- property: every public secret-roll commitment verifies against exactly one campaign-end preimage
- negative: changed scheme, seed, campaign salt, result, stream, index, or draw salt fails with a specific audit error
- negative: a result that hashes correctly but does not equal the re-derived seeded draw fails RNG verification
- privacy: preimages and lifted referee facts are unavailable from every live pre-end client view

## Done when
- campaign end produces a complete, deterministic black-box data artifact and a verifier independent of live projections; the artifact carries scheme/RNG versions, canonicalization rules, the seed preimage, and every draw preimage
- the view reports verified/failed counts and traces commitments to their ground-truth facts

## Do not
- no print-layout pipeline (M4) and no visibility mutation merely to render the one-time black box
