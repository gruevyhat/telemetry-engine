# M2-14 — Black-box preimage reveal and verification

## Tests first
- property: every public secret-roll commitment verifies against exactly one campaign-end preimage
- negative: changed result, stream, index, or salt fails with a specific audit error
- privacy: preimages and lifted referee facts are unavailable from every live pre-end client view

## Done when
- campaign end produces a complete, deterministic black-box data artifact and a verifier independent of live projections
- the view reports verified/failed counts and traces commitments to their ground-truth facts

## Do not
- no print-layout pipeline (M4) and no visibility mutation merely to render the one-time black box
